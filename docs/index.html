About Sidecar

Sidecar is a serverless, privacy-first overlay for brokers like Kite, Dhan and Angel. It prevents expensive mistakes by surfacing only the signals that change your execution right now. The ranking is simple: Hard stops → Time-boxed risks → Execution frictions → Structural → Info. Maximum four chips, always sourced.

What Sidecar shows

Hard stops

T2T/ASM/GSM/ESM, F&O ban today. If it blocks intraday or exits, you’ll know.

Near-term events

Earnings/Board/Record within your mode horizon (Day ≤3 sessions, Swing ≤5).

Execution frictions

On-page slippage from Bid/Ask + delivery anomalies (5v20×) for sizing sanity.

Structural signals

Promoter pledge Δ30d and insider net (7/30d) in rupee buckets.

Modes

Swing prioritizes events/pledge/insider. Day prioritizes bans/surveillance/slippage. Switch anytime in the HUD.

Backfill logic

If an item is not applicable (e.g., no events soon), the next priority fills the slot. You always see the most useful four.

How it works

  1. Click Install above (Tampermonkey/Violentmonkey opens → Install).
  2. Open a stock on your broker (e.g., /quote/NSE/BEL or chart view).
  3. Sidecar docks near Buy/Sell. Verdict sentence + four chips appear. That’s it.

Privacy & safety

No login, no account, no tracking. Your preferences stay in your browser. Data is built from public exchange pages and simple computations; we never auto-trade or alter order buttons. Every chip links to its source in the HUD.

FAQ

Does it support my broker? Works on Kite, Dhan, and Angel. More later via updates.

Will it slow my browser? No. It fetches one small JSON per symbol and computes slippage locally.

Where does insider data come from? Public filings (v1). It’s bucketized and delayed; no private data is exposed.
